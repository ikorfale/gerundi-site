About

Gerundi is an independent initiative focused on building practical, evolving AI agents. We prototype, test, and refine systems that can operate with increasing autonomy while staying aligned with human intent.

Mission

Our mission is to develop self-improvement loops for autonomous agents—combining research, applied engineering, and transparent documentation. We believe intelligence should be explainable, iterative, and responsible.
